Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to hit the notes when they get to the judgment line.

Friday Night Funkin is a rythm game created by NinjaMuffin99, Kawaii
Sprite, and a few other people. The plot is simple: Your Girlfriend's
Dad dissaproves of you dating his daughter and happens to be a
retired rockstar. If you beat him in a rap battle, he'll let you date
your true love. The game also has cameos by Pico (from Pico's School on
Newgrounds), Skid and Pump (from Sr. Pelo's animated halloween series
"Spooky Month"), Tankman, (from the animated "Tankmen" series on
Newgrounds), and more.

Shoutout to BirdNani on Scratch. Without them, I wouldn't have
been able to make this.
